About us

With a complete and varied menu, Cat's Café is a place for anytime, whether for breakfast, lunch, dinner or even Happy Hour. Our menu features award-winning espresso and coffee from Cat Milk Coffee and Cat Chocolate Coffee, pastries from Bread & Cie, cookies from The Cravory, and sandwiches from Sunshine & Orange. The cats are available for adoption, should you fall in love during your visit.
